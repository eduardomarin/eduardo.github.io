Wynn Casino Website's Facelift

Personal Project

Problem

One day I was looking for jobs at different casinos and after long hours of applying and going through numerous sites, I found myself somewhat annoyed. I didn't know what ticked me off the most: either the process of applying or the apathy and lack of care for these websites

Pain points:

- Visual discordance

- Content is everywhere, all over the place

- Pretty =/= Functional (These applies to the newer casinos )

Solution

Make it Visually appealing and Intuitive

- Work on a stable navigation

- Take out what seems to be redundant and test

- Keep it simple

Critique - Defining possible problems

These are just a few annotations of the things I thought were visually unappealing

Quick Solution for Space and Reservation form

Took over the screen rather than centering it and wasting some space on the sides. Also made the Reservation option accessible from popup



Add Ons for Instant Interaction

How did a user find what DJ is playing a XS before? He/she scanned the navigation, chose Nightlife this takes her to a page with a list of clubs.
