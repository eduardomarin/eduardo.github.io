Redesigning LowPriceDomains

role: ninja

Problem

Long ago, people got away with just throwing content at a website, with disregard of how it might be understood by the user. LowPriceDomains was this case, a "mashup of content" that didn't make much sense. While I changed a lot of its appereance, this wasn't the main focus. I listed the problems and condensed them into three main categories:
- Information Architecture
- Visual Appereance
- Search

Solution

Redesign, Rearchitect and Remodel the site

- Work on content planning
- Make it 'flat' (because that's cool)
- Optimize the way a user searches for domain names
