Freelance UX/UI designer for

littleBits

I joined littlebits for a short while as the third user experience designer. I focused mainly on revamping the Shop section and all the pages that constitute it. In my time there I got to create interchangeable, modular sections/patterns and contributed to a new, more concrete way of thinking about page structure.

Scoping out the Project


The task was rather broad: redesign all product pages in the Shop section. I had a style guide to follow, but I could also brainstorm and ideate new elements that could later be used across other pages.



Shop Page Components - Designing Modules


After identifying the core pieces used across product pages, I proceeded to create modular UI components that could be used interchangeably across different products: kits, bits, accessories and educators.



Shop Page - Home Page Layout


Once I had designed plenty of sections and iterated upon them, I began thinking about ways we could structure pages. I worked closely with the other designers and a few of the devs to figure out “how much we could change” in the following version.
