About Me

More than a Designer...
I’m a designer by heart for I like envisioning better solutions to our everyday interactions with new technologies.

With almost 3 years of experience designing and implementing websites, I have come across a few exciting projects from small businesses to startups to larger corporations. I graduated cum laude from St. John’s University with a degree in computer security systems. While doing a semester in Paris, where I worked as a resident assistant, I began learning about web design. A few months later I started freelancing. Although I started as a developer and now am proficient in the front-end, I find the architecture of an exceptional user interface far more interesting

I hope to work with an extremely intelligent, motivated and creative team of people, as I feel this would allow me to expand and progress as a creative thinker and designer.

In my free time: I play soccer, watch youtube videos and sketch for fun at times.
