About

My first job as a designer was at small, 6-person startup in Las Vegas. Since then, I’ve had the privilege of working for large corporations (30,000+ employees) as well as small to medium size companies/startups (50 to 1000 employees).

As a product designer, I am able to: conceptualize and strategize the requirements of a product, research (both qualitative and quantitative), and [lastly] build sound products through a good understanding of interaction and visual design principles.

My passion for design lies in the belief that we can solve the largest problems in society by taking a more user-centric approach to problem solving.

When I’m not designing I am: rock climbing, training my pup, and traveling.
